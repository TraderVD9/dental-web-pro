Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Who We Are

DentalWebAI is a web design service operated from the Netherlands. We build websites for dental practices worldwide.

Contact: hello@dentalwebai.com

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following data when you use our website or services:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email, clinic name, website URL
  • Audit requests: website URL you submit for analysis
  • Email correspondence: messages you send to hello@dentalwebai.com
  • Website analytics: anonymized page views (no personal tracking)

We do not collect: payment information (processed by Stripe), browsing history, cookies for tracking, or any health/medical data.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide website audits
  • To deliver web design services you've requested
  • To send follow-up communications about our services
  • To improve our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: when you submit a form or contact us
  • Legitimate interest: to improve our services and respond to inquiries
  • Contract: to deliver services you've purchased

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored on secure servers in the EU
  • Email is processed through Google Workspace (GDPR-compliant)
  • Website is hosted on GitHub Pages (Microsoft, GDPR-compliant)
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • We do not store credit card or payment information

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: restrict processing of your data
  • Portability: receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@dentalwebai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies for language preferences (stored in localStorage). We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Third-Party Services

  • GitHub Pages: website hosting
  • Cloudflare: DNS and email routing

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law. Contact form data is retained for 12 months unless you request deletion.
