## Discovery Call Talking Points

1. "How do most of your new patients find you right now?"
2. "When was the last time your website was updated?"
3. "Do you have online booking? What % book online vs. calling?"
4. "Are you happy with how your website represents your practice?"
5. "What would 10 more new patients per month mean for your revenue?"

Then share 2-3 specific findings from their website audit.
